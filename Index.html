OCEANIC PRESERVATION & AWARENESS CLUB

Our Mission

To spread awareness about how humans are destroying the world's beloved oceans and to educate people on the many ways in which we can help prevent the harmful effects of our behavior on the environment.

How?

Our goal is that you will leave our meetings every week having learned something new and feeling more motivated to make an impact. Come join us in room B212 every Wednesday during B-lunch and become involved in our many projects to help raise awareness!

Why?

Hundreds of thousands of creatures living in the ocean are affected in some way by our actions. Many die every year due to pollution of the ocean, overfishing, among other major problems. It is our responsibility to take care of the earth so that it may continue to take care of us.
